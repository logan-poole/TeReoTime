Te Reo Time — Privacy, Safety & Legal

Protecting tamariki, respecting Māori culture, and keeping your data safe.

Last updated: 21 November 2025 • Location: Aotearoa New Zealand

Quick Summary

Te Reo Time is a passion project created in Aotearoa New Zealand to support te reo Māori in everyday life. The app and this website are designed as safe, educational tools.

  • We do not require logins or user accounts.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact details through the app.
  • We do not show ads or sell data.
  • Any settings are stored on your device only, not on a server.

If how the app works changes in the future (e.g. accounts, online features, analytics), this page will be updated.

1. About this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Te Reo Time handles information when you use the app and visit any related webpages. It is written in plain language so whānau, schools, and tamariki can easily understand it.

This policy is intended to align with the principles of the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 and general good practice for children’s educational apps. It does not replace formal legal advice.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

When you use the Te Reo Time app, we do not ask you to provide:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or postal addresses
  • Payment or credit card information
  • Profile photos or personal media uploads
  • Contact lists or social media data
  • Precise GPS location

The app is designed to run locally on your device. Any learning progress or settings are stored on the device itself and are not sent to a central server controlled by Te Reo Time.

4. Information That May Be Processed Automatically

While Te Reo Time itself does not actively collect personal information, some technical data may be processed by third parties as part of normal app and website operation.

4.1. App Stores & Platform Providers

If you download Te Reo Time from an app store (for example Google Play or another store), that store may collect information such as:

  • Which country you downloaded the app from
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Basic install and crash statistics

This information is handled under the privacy policy of the app store provider (for example Google’s privacy policy). Te Reo Time only sees anonymised, aggregated statistics (for example total installs, crashes), not named individual user data.

4.2. Crash Reports & Diagnostics

Your device or app store may generate anonymous crash logs or error reports if the app stops working. These can help improve stability and remove bugs. They typically contain:

  • Anonymous device information (model, OS version)
  • Which screen or part of the app crashed
  • Technical error messages

Te Reo Time does not use this information to identify individual users.

4.3. Local Settings on Your Device

The app may store basic preferences directly on your device, for example:

  • Your preferred language or display mode
  • Which learning sections you’ve favourited
  • App settings such as sound or notification preferences

These settings stay on your device only and are deleted if you uninstall the app or clear its data.

5. Website, Cookies & Analytics

If you visit the Te Reo Time website or resources pages (for example an information site or documentation page), your web browser will automatically send some standard information, such as your IP address and browser type.

5.1. Cookies

At present, the Te Reo Time site:

  • Does not use tracking cookies for advertising.
  • May use basic cookies or local storage for things like remembering your theme (light/dark) or simple preferences.

5.2. Analytics

If simple analytics tools (for example privacy-friendly page view counters) are used, they are used only to understand:

  • How many people are visiting the site
  • Which pages are most useful
  • Which devices and browsers are being used (so the site can be improved)

These analytics are not used to build individual profiles and are not shared or sold. If more advanced analytics are added in the future, this section will be updated to explain exactly what is used and why.

6. Children & Young People

Te Reo Time is designed to be child-friendly and to support tamariki and whānau learning te reo Māori together.

  • The app does not require children to enter names, photos, or contact details.
  • The app does not include in-app purchases, ads, or social media sharing features.
  • Parents, caregivers, and kaiako are encouraged to supervise children’s device use.

If you are a parent, caregiver, or teacher and have any concerns about how Te Reo Time is used in your whānau or classroom, please get in touch using the contact details below.

7. How Any Information Is Used

Where any information is processed (for example anonymous usage data from an app store), it is used only to:

  • Monitor basic stability and performance of the app
  • Identify bugs and crashes that need fixing
  • Understand which features are most useful so they can be improved

Te Reo Time does not sell, rent, or trade user data to any third party.

8. Your Privacy Rights (Aotearoa New Zealand)

Under the principles of the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, if Te Reo Time ever holds personal information about you, you have the right to:

  • Ask whether any personal information about you is held
  • Request access to that information
  • Request correction of that information if it is wrong

At present, Te Reo Time is not designed to collect personally identifying information. If this changes, clear sign-posting and options will be added so you can make informed choices.

9. Security

Reasonable steps are taken to keep Te Reo Time and its related website secure, including:

  • Using reputable app stores and hosting providers
  • Keeping software and libraries up to date where possible
  • Designing the app to minimise the amount of personal data handled in the first place

No system can be 100% secure, but the aim is to reduce risk by collecting as little information as possible and keeping the app simple and local.

10. Cultural Care & Accuracy

Te Reo Time has been created with aroha and respect for te reo Māori, tikanga, and mātauranga Māori. However:

  • Content may not reflect every iwi or hapū variation.
  • Some explanations are simplified for learners.
  • There may be mistakes or areas that can be improved.

Where possible, users are encouraged to:

  • Seek guidance from local iwi, hapū, or Māori language experts
  • Use Te Reo Time as a starting point, not a final authority

If you see something that feels incorrect or inappropriate, please reach out so it can be reviewed and updated.
