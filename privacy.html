Privacy Policy
How we handle your information
No account • No tracking • NZ-based

Te Reo Time (“the App”, “we”, “our”, “us”) is an educational app that helps users learn Māori time phrases, maramataka (lunar phases), whakataukī, and daily cultural insights. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Device & App Information

We may collect basic, non-identifying information automatically, such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Anonymous usage analytics (if enabled)

This information helps improve performance and fix bugs. It is not used to personally identify you.

1.2 Location (Optional)

If you enable location permissions, the App may use:

  • Your approximate location to calculate sunrise/sunset times
  • Your city/region to show more relevant time information

Location data is only used on your device and is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

1.3 No Account Required

Te Reo Time does not require:

  • Sign-in or registration
  • Email address
  • Name or phone number
  • Any personally identifying information

We do not maintain user accounts or profiles.

1.4 Photos & Media (If Used)

If a feature allows you to choose images (for example, custom backgrounds), those images stay on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We only use the minimal data needed to:

  • Display Māori time and maramataka information correctly
  • Calculate sun and moon information (e.g., sunrise/sunset)
  • Improve app stability and performance

We do not sell, trade, or rent your data to anyone.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may connect to certain third-party services, for example:

  • Weather or astronomical data providers
  • Crash reporting or diagnostics via app stores

These providers may collect anonymous usage data under their own privacy policies. Te Reo Time does not send them any personal information such as your name, email, or address.

4. Data Storage & Security

We do not store your personal data on our own servers. Any settings and preferences are stored locally on your device.

We use standard security practices appropriate for a small, educational app, but no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

5. Children’s Privacy

Te Reo Time is safe and suitable for all ages. We intentionally do not collect personal information from children or adults. Because we do not collect identifiable data, there is nothing for us to sell, share, or track.

6. Your Choices & Rights

You can:

  • Disable location access at any time through your device settings
  • Uninstall the app, which removes all locally stored preferences
  • Contact us if you have any privacy questions

Since we do not maintain user accounts or central profiles, there is no stored personal data to request, export, or delete.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Significant changes may also be highlighted in the app or on the website at https://pinged.nz/TeReoTime/.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how Te Reo Time handles information, you can contact:

Te Reo Time provides educational content based on public knowledge, research, and guidance from Māori language and cultural resources where possible. However:

  • Māori language, maramataka, and tikanga vary between iwi, hapū, and rohe
